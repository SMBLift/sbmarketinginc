About Us

Our Story

S.B. Marketing was founded 15 years ago with the goal of making digital marketing accessible to businesses of all sizes.

The internet has undergone a large transformation since we opened in 2007.  In its earliest days, the internet wasn’t something many people understood or knew how to utilize, but it was something that intrigued us.  Many were reluctant to invest too much into it, but we saw the possibilities.  Now, most businesses know the importance of a strong online presence, but don’t necessarily know where to start.  Providing effective marketing solutions allows us to help our clients reach their fullest potential.  We believe in developing relationships with our partners and are known for our friendliness as much as our success rate.
